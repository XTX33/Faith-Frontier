    <h2>Summary</h2>
    <p>
      This civil matter arises from a complaint filed by the New Jersey Turnpike Authority
      seeking $5,109.45 in alleged toll-related damages against Devon T. Barber. Defendant
      filed an Answer, multiple motions (including a Motion to Dismiss), and subsequent
      pleadings. The matter was reinstated on October 27, 2025, following judicial review.
    </p>
