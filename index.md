-    <strong>due process, public record, and spiritual honesty</strong> ‚Äî to show what happens
-    not just in opinions and press releases, but in the filings, orders, and delays that shape
-    an ordinary life.
-  </p>

-    We believe justice should be accessible, understandable, and compassionate ‚Äî that the law
-    should be measured not only by what it permits, but by what it does to the people it touches.
